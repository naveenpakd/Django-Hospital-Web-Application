Emergency Services

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

For non-life-threatening emergencies, you can visit our hospital's emergency department. Our experienced medical staff is available 24/7 to provide urgent medical care.

Appointments

To schedule an appointment with one of our healthcare providers, please use our online appointment booking system or call our appointment hotline at [phone number].

We offer a wide range of medical services, including general medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, cardiology, orthopedics, and more. Our expert healthcare providers are committed to providing personalized care for all our patients.

Pharmacy

Our hospital has an in-house pharmacy that provides a wide range of prescription medications and over-the-counter products to meet the medication needs of our patients.

Our pharmacists are available to answer any questions you may have about your medications, provide medication counseling, and ensure that you are taking your medications safely and effectively.

Laboratory Services

Our hospital's laboratory services are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by highly skilled laboratory professionals who perform a wide range of diagnostic tests to support patient care.

We offer a comprehensive menu of laboratory tests, including blood tests, urine tests, microbiology, pathology, and more. Results are typically available within a timely manner to aid in diagnosis and treatment planning.

Billing

Our hospital's billing department is dedicated to helping patients understand their hospital bills and insurance coverage.

If you have questions about your hospital bill or need assistance with insurance claims, our billing team is available to assist you. You can contact our billing department at [phone number] or visit our hospital in person.
